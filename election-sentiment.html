Mapping the Emotional Landscape of Australian Elections

The Story Behind the Numbers

Beyond the polling numbers and policy debates, elections are driven by the emotional pulse of the nation. This analysis visualizes the sentiment surrounding Australian federal elections, revealing how public mood, key issues, and campaign messaging shape electoral outcomes.

Key Insights

Issue Evolution

How public sentiment around key issues evolves throughout the campaign, with surprising shifts in what matters most to voters.

Regional Variations

Distinct patterns in how different regions respond to campaign messaging, revealing the complex geography of Australian politics.

Campaign Impact

The measurable effect of campaign events, debates, and announcements on public sentiment and voting intentions.

Media Influence

How different media sources and coverage patterns shape public perception and electoral outcomes.
